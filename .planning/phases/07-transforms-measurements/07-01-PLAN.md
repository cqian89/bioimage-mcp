- `IMAGE_AND_LABELS_TO_JSON` -> inputs: `image: BioImageRef`, `labels: LabelImageRef`; outputs: `output: NativeOutputRef` with `format: json`
- `IMAGE_TO_LABELS_AND_JSON` -> inputs: `image: BioImageRef`; outputs: `labels: LabelImageRef` and `counts: NativeOutputRef` with `format: json`

Keep existing patterns unchanged.
  </action>
  <verify>python -m compileall src/bioimage_mcp/registry/dynamic/models.py src/bioimage_mcp/registry/loader.py</verify>
