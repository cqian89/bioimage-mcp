## Problem

The current test suite is large and contains many legacy tests that no longer reflect the project's architecture or requirements. This creates a significant maintenance burden, slows down development, and makes it difficult to ensure coverage for new features in the rapidly evolving codebase.

## Solution

Research and develop a strategy for test consolidation and cleanup. This includes:
- Auditing the existing test suite (unit, contract, integration, smoke).
- Identifying redundant or obsolete tests.
- Proposing a new structure that aligns with the current v0.4.x architecture.
- Executing the consolidation to reduce maintenance overhead while maintaining high reliability.
